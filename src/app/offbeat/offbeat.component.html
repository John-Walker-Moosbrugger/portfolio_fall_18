OffBeat

Off-Beat was the culmination of a semester long Service Design project. Service design is a framework of design thinking which is unique in that it considers all parts of an organization as part of the design. For example, a service designer working for a restaurant chain would consider far more than normal customer experiences and touchpoints (waitstaff, menuâ€™s, ect.). Their design would include the backstage processes, from how food is delivered to how the kitchen staff receives the orders in order to provide the best experience to all stakeholders.

For this project we were tasked with designing a music related service that targeted an audience other than the mass market.
